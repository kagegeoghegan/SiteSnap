SiteSnap: Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

SiteSnap: Pro ("the App") is operated by an individual developer. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The App stores all project data — photos, videos, documents, annotations, and map plans — locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

When you sign in to Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive, the App requests access to your cloud storage solely to upload and organize your project files. We do not read, modify, or access any files outside of folders you explicitly select.

Location data is used to geotag photos and display your position on the in-app map. Location is never transmitted off your device by the App.

Third-Party Services

Google Sign-In and the Google Drive API are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Microsoft authentication and the OneDrive API are governed by Microsoft's Privacy Policy. The App does not share your data with any other third parties.

Data Retention

All project data lives on your device and in any cloud folders you choose to sync. Deleting the App removes all locally stored data. Cloud copies are managed by you directly through Google Drive or OneDrive.
